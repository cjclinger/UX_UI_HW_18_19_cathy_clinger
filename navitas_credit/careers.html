JOIN THE NAVITAS TEAM!

Is This What You Are Looking For In A Company?

Want to make a real impact and are tired of just having a job? We are a small but rapidly growing company…you have a true opportunity to personally contribute to our success and your career growth at the same time.

 

The Navitas environment is entrepreneurial and fast pace. Never a dull day…or minute. You are encouraged to contribute your ideas and thoughts, everyone has a stake in our success. If we do well as an organization, you will personally benefit financially. We are always looking for talented individuals in the finance, accounting, customer service and sales fields.

 

We pride ourselves on our team based environment, where everyone knows their goals, what we need to achieve as an organization, and how the company is performing. Our culture of sharing information, good or bad, keeps everyone fully aware and focused on what needs to be done to achieve the desired results.

 

We offer highly competitive compensation plans, company performance bonuses, extensive health benefit options, and an employer match 401K plan. Rewarding careers that reward you for performance, a win/win proposition for you and Navitas Credit Corp.
