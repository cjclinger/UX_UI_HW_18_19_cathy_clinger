PRIVACY POLICY

GENERAL

Navitas Credit Corp. (“Navitas”, “we”, “our” or “us”) recognizes the importance of your privacy and protecting the confidential information that you may choose to send us, whether by email, regular mail, in–person delivery, fax or phone, or through our Sites and Applications. Our “Sites and Applications” include the following website, customer portals, and dealer portals:

PROTECTING YOUR PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION IS EXTREMELY IMPORTANT TO US. Our company is a member of the Better Business Bureau and strives to act in a professional and ethical manner in all of our business dealings. Our company’s Privacy Policy is set forth below. This policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by us and how it is used.

 

USING NAVITAS’ SITES AND APPLICATIONS

The terms of this Privacy Policy are incorporated into the separate Terms of Use governing your use of our Sites and Applications. You automatically agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy by using our Sites and Applications. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THESE PRIVACY TERMS, DO NOT USE OUR SITES AND APPLICATIONS.

 

Log Files

Navitas makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

 

DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - http://www.google.com/privacy ads.html. Advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our sites. Advertising partners include third-party ad servers or ad networks that use technology in the advertisements and links that appear on our sites sent directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see. We have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

 

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Our Privacy Policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

 

Personal and Business Information

We collect personal and business information you voluntarily choose to disclose to us, whether over the internet or our website, or by email, regular mail, in–person delivery, fax or phone. This information is collected and maintained for appropriate business reasons. We utilize this information for a variety of reasons, including but not limited to, to:

  • verify your identity as our customer
  • evaluate credit applications submitted by you or an authorized party to us
  • establish and manage customer accounts
  • complete customer transactions
  • respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests
  • understand customer needs
  • market products and services
  • improve our Sites and Applications
  • control risk, including but not limited to maintaining information security, and combating fraud
  • track usage (such as number of hits, pages visited and the length of user sessions) in order to evaluate the usefulness of our Sites and Applications
  • respond to interests of employment with us
  • comply with legal and regulatory requirements and with other legal process and law enforcement requirement
  • conduct other business activities, including but not limited to data analysis, audits, product and services improvements, and enhancement
  • take any other lawful and appropriate actions regarding your relationship with us

 

If you choose to correspond with us, we may retain the content of your messages together with your address, fax number, email address and/or our responses. You may also provide us information about yourself, your firm or company, and your practices when you communicate with us through any means. Examples of such information include nonpublic personal and business financial information on contact forms, vendor applications and credit applications. Examples of the categories of nonpublic personally identifiable financial information that we maintain concerning you include, but are not limited to:

  • Information we receive from you or an authorized third party on applications or other forms, including consumer and commercial reports you authorize us to obtain
  • Information about your transactions with us
  • Information on banking accounts
  • Credit and payment history
  • Whether you own or rent your residence and the length of time that you have resided at your current address
  • Tax returns
  • Financial statements and information

 

Sharing of Information with Third Parties

Navitas may share the information you choose to provide us with its various suppliers, affiliates, business and financial partners, financing sources, equipment vendors who might have referred you to us, and with our affiliates (collectively, the “Third Parties”), for any of the purposes set forth in the “Personal and Business Information” section above. We expect our Third Party providers to exercise the same degree of care regarding your information that we exercise. As we develop our business, we may also transfer information you have provided to us in the course of sales of assets, corporate divestitures, mergers, acquisitions, or any dissolution.

 

Inaccurate Information

Please contact us immediately if you believe that our records contain inaccurate or incomplete information about you or your company. We will review your concerns and correct our records if necessary.

 

Security of Information

Personnel at our company have access to any confidential information that you chose to provide us. Information that resides on our various operating systems is password protected. All company personnel sign confidentiality agreements regarding the proper use of your information.

 

Children

Our Sites and Applications are not intended for visitors under 18 years of age. Navitas does not knowingly solicit any information from, or market its services or products to, children.

 

Changes to our Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy is subject to periodic review and changes. If we materially change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes to this Privacy Policy for you to view. When we do make changes, we will revise the “Effective” date at the top of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal and confidential information we collect. Your continued use of our website and the products and services we provide constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates.

 

Authorization and Consent to Use Information

By providing information to Navitas through our Sites and Applications or by e-mail, fax, regular mail, in-person delivery or phone, you authorize our use of that information for the purposes set forth in the “Personal and Business Information” set forth above. We may also use the information we collect to contact you about updates to our website, new services and special offers we think you will find valuable.

 

Please direct any questions regarding this policy to: info@navitascredit.com.
