PARTNER FUNDING

THIRD PARTY ORIGINATORS COMMAND HIGHER CLOSING RATES WITH NAVITAS

The Partner Funding Division of Navitas Credit Corp. delivers highly customized financing solutions to the broker community that reflect the needs of their local markets and customers. Our unmatched experience and understanding of the industry aids qualified originators to maximize their closing rates with creative products and services. We specialize in providing financing for transactions from as low as $5,000 up to $750,000.

PARTNER WITH LENDING EXPERTS THAT KNOW YOUR BUSINESS

Putting our wide range of expertise to work for you, we have specialized teams that understand the specific types of equipment and unique industries you and your vendors service. We have designed our organization with two highly specialized teams based on the equipment and credit profile of your customers. This unique structure not only enables us to expedite the processing of your transactions, it also ensures you receive the best credit decisions each and every time.

ASSET BASED LENDING TEAM

This team specializes in underwriting transactions based on the quality and value of the asset. The analysts in this group possess the knowledge and expertise to support an extensive list of equipment categories ranging from construction, industrial, and car wash equipment to specialty titled vehicles.

 

Specific “collateral expertise” enables this group to better assess and understand the credit risk profiles associated with these equipment categories. They know and understand the equipment and the end users that utilize these assets.

CREDIT BASED LENDING TEAM

Our seasoned analysts evaluate transactions financing collateral with limited or no collateral value. These “soft asset” submittals require highly specialized understanding and expertise in an extensive list of industries that range from service or software providers, medical, HOAs, energy retrofit, and equipment rental, to retail petroleum, and restaurants.

 

Almost any type of equipment, regardless of collateral “value”, can be financed for established businesses. Decisions are based on time in business, debt servicing history, and reasonable credit quality. Unique programs are developed for you to provide your vendors or to serve a specific industry.
