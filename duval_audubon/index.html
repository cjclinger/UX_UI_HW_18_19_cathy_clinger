Duval Audubon Society

Connecting people with nature, conserving and restoring natural ecosystems, focusing on birds and other wildlife.

Serving Clay, Duval and Nassau Counties

Welcome to Duval Audubon Society, a chapter of the National Audubon Society in Northeast Florida. With over 1,350 members and supporters from Clay, Duval, and Nassau Counties, we host monthly informational meetings, host special events, and offer volunteer opportunities.

Conservation

By protecting birds, we are also safeguarding the Western hemisphere's great natural heritage for future generations, preserving our shared quality of life and fostering a healthier environment for us all.

Advocacy

Audubon is a respected and influential voice on public policy issues, from town halls to the U.S. Capitol. We have the power to convene diverse stakeholders to solve even the toughest problems.

Engagement

The way to create the next generation of conservationist is to instill the love of bird watching in them by creating fun & educational events while engaging with like minded people. Volunteer and take action with Duval.

Get involved. Together, we can make a difference!
